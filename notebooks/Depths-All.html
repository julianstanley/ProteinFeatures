How much variability in depth do we see per protein and per aa?¶

Answer: It depends...but we can look at some histograms to get an idea.

In [7]:
depths_variation = {}
for aa_pos_prot in depths["AA-Position-Protein"].unique():
    these_depths = depths[depths["AA-Position-Protein"] == aa_pos_prot]['depth']
    depth_var = np.std(these_depths)
    
    protein = aa_pos_prot.split(':')[2]
    if protein in depths_variation:
        depths_variation[protein].append(depth_var)
    else:
        depths_variation[protein] = [depth_var]
